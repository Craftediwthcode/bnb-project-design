Top 10 Things to Do in Bradenton, FL

Bradenton, FL, offers a mix of cultural attractions, outdoor adventures, and historic charm. Here’s a guide to the top 10 things to do in this vibrant city:

  1. Riverwalk
    Stroll along the scenic Riverwalk with its beautiful waterfront views, playgrounds, and public art installations.

  2. South Florida Museum
    Explore exhibits on local history, archaeology, and the natural sciences, and meet Snooty the manatee.

  3. Village of the Arts
    Visit Florida's largest live-work arts community to admire galleries, enjoy live performances, and dine in eclectic cafes.

  4. Robinson Preserve
    This stunning nature preserve offers kayaking, biking, and walking trails with abundant wildlife and scenic vistas.

  5. Bradenton Beach
    Relax on soft sands or enjoy water sports at this beautiful Gulf Coast beach.

  6. DeSoto National Memorial
    Step back in time at this historic site, commemorating the landing of Hernando de Soto in 1539.

  7. Mixon Fruit Farms
    Take a tram tour of this family-run citrus grove, sample fresh fruit, and learn about Florida’s agriculture.

  8. Manatee Performing Arts Center
    Catch a live theater performance or concert at this cultural hub.

  9. Shopping at Ellenton Premium Outlets
    Enjoy some retail therapy at this popular outlet mall featuring top brands.

  10. Breweries and Wineries
    Sample craft beer at Motorworks Brewing or sip local wines at Fiorelli Winery.
