About The Hosts

BNB Gulf Coast started with Randy & Barb and their mission to provide great experiences for guests visiting our beautiful beaches and all this gorgeous Gulf Coast has to offer.
Their focus on hospitality that rises far above just providing a property for guests to stay at, their attention to detail and their commitment to each guest, has set them apart as leaders in this industry that is growing day by day.
With a focus on family fun, that have designed their homes with the comfort of the coast while also creating spaces that are overflowing with interactive opportunities for families and friends alike, to enjoy precious time together. Randy and Barb love this area and what they do.

They look forward to the opportunity to WOW you, and help you experience The Gulf Coast in a way that keeps you returning year after year.

Our Team

To maintain their 5 Star Super Host status, BNB Gulf Coast has assembled a team of employees who are equally committed to your unforgettable experience in and on our properties.

They employee their own support/management staff, cleaners, maintenance staff, pool maintenance and landscapers, who work to ensure their properties are meticulously maintained and prepared for your arrival.

Why we chose Bradenton and Sarasota, FLorida?

Nestled along Florida’s Gulf Coast, Bradenton and Sarasota captivate with their unparalleled charm, stunning beaches, and vibrant cultural scene.
From the powdery sands of Siesta Key and Anna Maria Island to the quaint streets of downtown Bradenton, this region offers something for everyone.

We chose Bradenton and Sarasota for their perfect blend of natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and rich cultural heritage, providing an ideal backdrop for our guests to unwind, explore, and create lasting memories.

Whether you’re seeking relaxation by the shore or excitement in the city, our properties offer the perfect starting point for your Gulf Coast adventure.
